Privacy Policy

ASL Floorfy Image Counter & Report Generator
Last updated: December 04, 2025

Important: This Chrome extension is private and exclusively for internal use by employees and authorized agents of our organization (ASL Preservation Solutions). It is not intended for public or third-party use.

All users (agents/photographers) are informed in advance and explicitly agree that their work data will be collected and stored through this extension for internal quality control and performance monitoring purposes.

1. Data We Collect (Only When Using the Extension on Floorfy)

  • Tour Number – The unique Floorfy tour identifier (we do not collect or store the full URL)
  • Number of images generated for that tour
  • Employee / Agent name – Automatically taken from the name associated with your Google account (the one you use to log in to Google Sheets)
  • Date and time of the entry

This data is used solely to analyze employee quality, creativity, and productivity within our organization.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

The only purpose of collecting this information is internal performance monitoring and quality assurance of virtual tours created for ASL Preservation Solutions. No data is shared with any external parties.

3. Google Sheets Integration (Internal Company Sheet)

  • When you first connect the extension, you authorize it (via Google OAuth) to append rows to our company’s private Google Sheet.
  • The extension only has permission to add new rows – it cannot read, edit, or delete existing data.
  • Your Google credentials are never stored. Authentication is handled securely and directly by Google.

4. Local Storage on Your Device

The extension temporarily stores your recent work history locally in the browser (Chrome extension storage) so you can:

  • View your entries inside the extension popup
  • Download them as a CSV file for your own records
This local data is only accessible on your computer and is automatically deleted if you uninstall the extension or clear browser data.

5. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Full Floorfy tour URLs
  • Any photos, videos, or property details
  • Personal contact information beyond your Google account display name
  • Browsing activity outside of floorfy.com
  • Any analytics or tracking for advertising purposes

6. Who Has Access to the Data

Only authorized management personnel at ASL Preservation Solutions have access to the company Google Sheet for internal review and payroll/quality purposes.

7. Permissions Explained

  • storage – Save your settings and recent work history locally
  • https://*.floorfy.com/* – Read the tour number and image count
  • https://sheets.googleapis.com/* – Append rows to the company sheet
  • identity – Get your Google account name (display name only)
  • downloads – Allow CSV export

8. Data Security

All communication with Google Sheets uses HTTPS and official Google OAuth 2.0. No data passes through any third-party server.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be communicated to all users and the updated version will be published at this URL.
