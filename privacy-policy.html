Privacy Policy

ASL Floorfy Image Counter & Report Generator
Last updated: December 04, 2025

Important: This is a private, internal-use-only Chrome extension developed exclusively for employees and authorized agents of ASL Preservation Solutions. It is not distributed publicly.

All users are informed and agree that their work data is collected through this tool for internal quality and performance monitoring.

1. Data We Collect (Only on Floorfy Pages)

  • Tour Number (we never collect or store the full URL)
  • Number of images generated for that tour
  • Employee / Agent name – Automatically taken from your Google account display name
  • Date and time of the entry

Purpose: Internal analysis of employee quality, creativity, and productivity.

2. Google Sheets Integration

The extension appends the above data directly to the company’s private Google Sheet (via secure Google OAuth). It only has permission to add new rows — it cannot read or modify existing data.

3. Use of chrome.storage.sync (Why We Need the "storage" Permission)

The storage permission is required to use chrome.storage.sync. This allows the following essential settings to automatically synchronize across all your Chrome browsers when you’re signed in with your Google account:

  • Your employee name
  • The Google Sheet ID / URL used for company reporting
  • Other minor preferences (e.g., UI settings)

This ensures the extension works consistently and correctly no matter which company computer or profile you use. Only these configuration settings are synced — no work history or tour data is stored in chrome.storage.sync.

4. Local Work History (chrome.storage.local)

Your recent entries are temporarily saved locally on the device (using chrome.storage.local) so you can:

  • View your list inside the extension popup
  • Download it as a CSV file
This local data is never synced or shared and is deleted when you uninstall the extension or clear browser data.

5. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Full Floorfy tour URLs
  • Photos, videos, or property details
  • Personal contact information beyond your Google display name
  • Browsing activity outside floorfy.com
  • Any analytics or advertising tracking

6. Permissions Summary

  • storage – Save and sync settings + temporary local work history
  • https://*.floorfy.com/* – Read tour number and image count
  • https://sheets.googleapis.com/* – Append rows to company sheet
  • identity – Retrieve your Google account display name
  • downloads – Export CSV reports

7. Data Security & Access

All connections use HTTPS and official Google OAuth. Only authorized ASL Preservation Solutions management can access the company Google Sheet.
